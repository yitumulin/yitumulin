About

I treat this website as a long-term technical portfolio.

I study at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (International School, Telecommunication Engineering and Management). This site is where I publish AI/LLM projects, research notes, and product engineering iterations.

Technical Direction

My core focus is the full path from research to production: not just model experiments, but also robust UX, implementation tradeoffs, and maintainable architecture.

Upcoming work here will center on LLM systems: prompt strategy, RAG/agent workflows, inference and deployment design, and practical product scenarios.

For every project I try to answer three things clearly: what problem it solves, what approach is used, and why that approach is technically reasonable.
